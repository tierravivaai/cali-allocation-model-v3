# Option 2: Banded IUSAF — IPLC Allocation for Developed Country Parties

Under the banded approach, 9 developed countries are added to the
IUSAF band structure alongside the existing eligible Parties.
Their hypothetical share is determined by their band weight.
TSAC and SOSAC are both zero.

**Band assignments (2027 UN Scale):**
- Band 4 (0.1%–1.0%): Denmark, Finland, New Zealand, Norway, Sweden
- Band 5 (1.0%–10.0%): Australia, Canada, Japan, Russian Federation

**Assumptions:**
- IPLC / State split: 50 / 50
- TSAC = 0%, SOSAC = 0%
- Band-based inversion (un_scale_mode = band_inversion)
- Only the 9 specified developed countries are added as eligible; all other
  high-income CBD Parties remain excluded
